The Cloud can be a daunting place.  It’s filled with complexity, terminology and unfamiliarity along with the task of deciding which cloud to use.   But, it may also be your best option when it comes to building mature software projects with its various runtimes, Services, Containers and advanced deployment techniques like Continuous Delivery. The value to you and your application can be tremendous! 

 

If words like Services or Continuous Delivery initiates a bit of anxiety; don’t worry, our Quick2Cloud College, with its quick Courses, will explain everything using interactive examples, diagrams and script generations guaranteeing that you will be making use of the Cloud before too long.

 

Quick2Cloud: provides both knowledge and assistance in getting your application quickly into one of the several Clouds available; all you have to do is decide which Cloud to start with. 

 

 Then, once deployed, you can deploy into another Cloud, trying all the Clouds for functionality. 

 

And, with the Quick2Cloud CloudShop feature, you can determine which Cloud is feature-wise best for your needs.  To get cross-Cloud functionality why not try q2cBridge, a way to deploy some applications in one Cloud and some Services in another with the results giving the best of all Clouds. 

 

It took much time to acquire the knowledge of the many Clouds inner-workings with the hope that Quick2Cloud can transfer some basic knowledge to you in a very quick and simple way then you can feel more confident in trying more advanced features as your experience grows.
